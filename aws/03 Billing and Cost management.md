## Bills
in the billing and cost management home page under Billing and payments on the left nav go to bills
there we can see charge by service after total active services
we can see how much of our free tier has been exhausted under cost analysis
## Budgets
in the billing and cost management home page under Budgets and planning on the left nav go to budgets 
create budgets to get alerted when we reach thresholds
we can create a budget with the zero cost template that will alert us as soon as we hit $0.01 on our free account
we can add emails that should be notified when the budget is hit
we have other templates for Monthly cost etc
monthly cost notifies you when you reach 85% of actual spend or forecasted spend reaches 100% or the actual spend becomes 100%
